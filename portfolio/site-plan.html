Overview

Purpose

Are you a Last Airbender fan? McQuinn Belnap is an illustrator, and he drew the Avatar characters into an Avengers poster. Would you like to see it? It’d be great to have a place people could view McQuinn's illustrations outside of social media. Having an online portfolio to showcase his work would provide a place for anyone interested to view his illustrative pieces. With a website independent of his Facebook and Instagram profile, people could have more convenient access to his storefront.

Audience

Anyone interested in seeing McQuinn Belnap's illustrative work

Content

Home page

*for my personal site, I have elected to keep it very minimal in text information, as it has been suggested by several professors that a portfolio website for artists should get potential clients directly to the artist's work. I wont' include this in the content of the actual website, but I will answer these questions in hopes of fulfilling the necessary requirements of the W08 assignment.

Why will people come to my site?

To see my illustrative portfolio

What questions will your visitors expect my site to answer for them?

What has McQuinn illustrated? What style of art does McQuinn create? Where can buy McQuinn's tshirts? What is McQuinn's email?

What types of activities will vistors want ot complete on my site?

Portfolio viewing and store accessing

What information is necessary to complete any actions my site offers?

Pictures of my illustrations, my email address, and some about info

Why will my vistors come to my site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

Uploading my illustrations in this format will give a better viewing experience for visitors, seeing how the image files are lossless.

About page

I’m a bachelor of the arts in art.
And I’m construction worker.
And I just LOVE to draw.

I love to draw because I love progress. I love seeing an image come to fruition on a 2D surface. Drawing is an avenue through which we can give a physical form to ideas. Drawing lets us explore the universe. It can take us to places with lions, ligers, and bears…and an alien mage with a 47-foot-long tail.
